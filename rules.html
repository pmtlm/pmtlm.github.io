RULES AND REGULATION

  • Malaysia Citizenship Only
  • This Tournament Will Be Held 1 Month And 3 Days Every Week
  • Applicable To All Age
  • Number Of Player
  • Maximum 4 Player
  • Last Register Before 1st January 2021
  • Tournament Will Be Held On 1st February 2021
  • First 100 Team That Register Can Enter The Tournament

WEEK DESCRIPTION
Week 1 -100 Teams will divides into 4 group
-25 Teams in each group will compete
Week 2 -25 Teams in each group will compete
-At the end of this week, the top 10 team in each group with the most point will qualified into the next round in the next week.
Week 3 -Top 10 team of each group will be divided into 2 group
-20 Teams in each group will compete
-At the end of this week, the top 10 team in each group with the most point will qualified into final round in the next week.
week 4 -20 Teams that qualified will compete in one group
-The winner will be calculated based on their last position points and kill points
